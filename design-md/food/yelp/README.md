# Yelp — iOS design spec

Local business reviews. One rationed Yelp Red `#FF1A1A` (logo `#D32323`) for the primary CTA + active tab, a warm orange-red five-star rating `#F25C05` (identical light and dark, half-star increments) as the brand asset, a full-bleed business photo header with a "1 / 248 photos" counter, Recommended Reviews cards with Useful/Funny/Cool votes, 8px gray `#F0F0F0` / dark `#0C0C0C` section bands instead of cards, and green/red `#2DA44E`/`#E03E3E` open-closed status on a near-black `#161616` dark canvas

The complete **Yelp** design system — free. The framework-neutral `DESIGN.md` plus SwiftUI, Expo, and Jetpack Compose companions: screen-by-screen breakdown, full color & type system, component specs, motion curves, haptics, and a ready-to-paste AI-agent implementation prompt.
