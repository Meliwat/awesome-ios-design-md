# Social — design specs

23 apps. Browse every screen free in the [live gallery](https://www.spectr.to/gallery/social).

- [**Instagram**](instagram/) — Photo & video social. Monochrome chrome, 10-stop brand gradient reserved for Stories + Create, icon-only tab bar, true-black OLED dark mode
- [**TikTok**](tiktok/) — Short-form video. Full-bleed vertical FYP, chromatic-aberration logo (cyan + red), right-side action rail, signature glitch aesthetic
- [**X (Twitter)**](x-twitter/) — Real-time text-first social. Pure black OLED canvas, X Blue `#1D9BF0` reserved for links, Chirp font, column-centered feed, per-action color micro-animations
- [**Threads**](threads/) — Text-forward Meta social. Pure black canvas, Instagram-coral like heart, thread-line indentation, no hashtags/trending, Instagram Sans
- [**Facebook**](facebook/) — Social network. Facebook Blue `#1877F2`, `#F0F2F5` feed background, 7-emoji Reactions popover with drag-select, profile cover hero
- [**Snapchat**](snapchat/) — Camera-first social. Snap Yellow `#FFFC00`, 5-screen swipe nav, 82pt yellow capture button, color-coded snap types, Avenir Next
- [**Reddit**](reddit/) — Communities & discussion. Reddit Orange-Red `#FF4500`, upvote-orange `#FF8717` / downvote `#7193FF` vote column, flat post cards, 10pt-per-level comment indentation
- [**Pinterest**](pinterest/) — Visual discovery. Pinterest Red `#E60023`, masonry 2-column grid with variable heights, floating Save-button morph, Pinterest Sans
- [**LinkedIn**](linkedin/) — Professional network. Cream canvas `#F3F2EF`, LinkedIn Blue `#0A66C2`, 6-reaction picker, Premium gold avatar frame, Open-to-Work green ring
- [**BeReal**](bereal/) — Authentic dual-camera social. Stark pure-black `#000000` canvas, no brand accent (white-only), dual front+back composite card, 2-minute countdown, RealMojis, zero vanity metrics
- [**Mastodon**](mastodon/) — Federated social. Mastodon Purple `#6364FF`, `@user@instance` handles, content-warning spoilers, home/local/federated timelines, boost-with-spin, light + `#191B22` dark
- [**Bluesky**](bluesky/) — Custom-feeds social. Bluesky Blue `#1185FE`, butterfly mark, switchable pinned custom feeds, reply-control chips, three themes (light / dim `#1E2936` / dark `#0B0F14`)
- [**Nextdoor**](nextdoor/) — Hyperlocal community. Nextdoor Green `#00B246`, warm cream `#FAF9F6` canvas, neighborhood feed, map-with-pins, verified-neighbor badge, Lato
- [**Tumblr**](tumblr/) — Creative blogging social. Deep-navy `#001935` canvas, multi-accent trio (blue `#00B8FF` / green `#00CF35` / pink `#FF4930`), reblog chains, tag bar, notes count
- [**Quora**](quora/) — Q&A knowledge. Quora Red `#B92B27`, serif-question / sans-answer split (Georgia + Inter), blue `#2E69FF` upvote pill, credential bylines, Spaces
- [**VSCO**](vsco/) — film-grade photo & video editor. Pure-black darkroom canvas (`#000000`), monochrome chrome with hairline bipolar sliders on a `#3A3A3A` track, a white-ringed film-preset carousel, and UPPERCASE wide-tracked Inter labels — zero brand accent, the photo is the only color.
- [**Clubhouse**](clubhouse/) — live-audio social rooms. Warm cream "hallway" canvas (`#F2EFE4`) with DM Serif room titles, a grid of circular speaker avatars, and an emerald speaking-pulse ring (`#38B569` + `rgba(56,181,105,0.18)` halo) — all-pill controls, warm `#1A1A1A` dark mode, no blue anywhere.
- [**Lemon8**](lemon8/) — Lifestyle & social discovery. Bright editorial white canvas (`#FFFFFF`), two-column staggered masonry feed of magazine-cover cards, sparing Lemon8 Yellow (`#FFE600`) paired only with ink `#1A1A1A`, and a pastel topic-tag system (mint `#E4F5EC`, blush `#FCE8EE`, sky `#E6F0FB`).
- [**Flickr**](flickr/) — Photo sharing & community. Near-black gallery canvas (`#0C0D0E`), justified photo mosaic with 3pt gutters, twin brand dots (Pink `#FF0084` + Blue `#0063DC`), and a monospace EXIF camera-detail table.
- [**Vimeo**](vimeo/) — cinematic creator video platform. Near-black screening-room canvas (#0D0E12) with a single Vimeo Blue accent (#00ADEF), the iconic Staff Pick gold star (#FFD24C), and a 14pt-corner auto-fading player.
- [**Vero**](vero/) — calm, no-algorithm chronological social. Pure true-black canvas (#000000) with a single teal-to-blue brand gradient (#00D1C1 to #0079D3), flat teal accents (#00C2B8), and a 7 first-class post-type selector.
- [**Gas**](gas/) — joyful anonymous compliments for schools. Full-bleed indigo-to-purple gradient (#6C5CE7 to #A06CFF) behind a big white poll card, four fixed festive choice colors, and the flame #FF7A45 currency loop.
- [**Locket**](locket/) — warm, widget-first photos from your closest friends. Cream-gold world (#FFF7EC / #FFB02E) with a square full-bleed friend photo, a big white capture button with a gold ring, and a 4-up photo-history grid.
