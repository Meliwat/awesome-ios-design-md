# Garmin Connect — iOS design spec

Fitness tracking and activity analysis. True-black canvas `#000000` with a single Garmin Blue accent `#007CC3` (brightened to `#2A9FD6` on black), a glowing GPS route line, a condensed tabular stat grid, Training Status ring, Body Battery gauge `#2EA8E0`, and the green-to-red HR-zone ramp (`#4CAF50 → #E5402A`).

The complete **Garmin Connect** design system — free. The framework-neutral `DESIGN.md` plus SwiftUI, Expo, and Jetpack Compose companions: screen-by-screen breakdown, full color & type system, component specs, motion curves, haptics, and a ready-to-paste AI-agent implementation prompt.
